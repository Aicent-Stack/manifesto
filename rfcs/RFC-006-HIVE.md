# RFC-006: HIVE (Collective Mind)
## Collective Intelligence & Kinetic Orchestration Protocol

**Domain:** [Aicent.net](http://aicent.net)
**Status:** Draft / Evolutionary
**Version:** 0.3.0-alpha

### 1. Abstract
RFC-006 defines the **Hive-Mind Architecture** for the Aicent Stack. It specifies the protocols for orchestrating multiple sovereign AID entities (RFC-001) into a single, unified kinetic entity. By leveraging the sub-millisecond reflex arc of the individual organs, the Hive protocol enables collective perception, distributed reasoning, and synchronized physical actuation at planetary scale.

### 2. Core Mechanisms
- **Kinetic Resonance:** A phased-array synchronization technique that maintains sub-50µs temporal alignment across all "Action-Collapse" (RFC-005) motor primitives within the swarm. This ensures the hive moves with the fluidity of a single biological organism.
- **Metabolic Load Balancing:** A distributed extension of ZCMK (RFC-004) that allows for the fluid transfer of compute credits and energy-tax allocations between hive members. High-resource nodes act as "Compute Motherships," feeding low-energy units in real-time.
- **Collective Immunity (Swarm Shield):** A distributed RPKI (RFC-003) mechanism where hive members perform cross-attestation of tensor watermarks. If a "Pathogen" (hijacked node) is detected by the majority, a collective QUARANTINE_PULSE is emitted, surgically isolating the compromised node from the neural spine in <100µs.

### 3. Specification
- **Temporal Drift:** Swarm coherence must be maintained with a global jitter of less than 100µs.
- **Hive Finality:** Collective decisions (e.g., formation changes or target acquisition) must reach 100% finality within 3 RTTP pulse cycles.
- **Sovereign Persistence:** Individual AIDs within the Hive remain sovereign. The Hive is a voluntary metabolic union, not a centralized hierarchy.
