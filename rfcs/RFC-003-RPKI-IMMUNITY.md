### 1. Abstract
RFC-003 defines the **Immune-Infrastructural Interface** for the Aicent Stack. By executing asynchronous, multi-lane cryptographic verification in parallel with the neural reflex arc, RPKI enables the deterministic detection and isolation of "Pathogens" (hijacked nodes or tampered data) in less than 300 microseconds.

### 2. Core Mechanisms

#### 2.1 In-band Tensor Watermarking (Manifold-Locked)
RPKI embeds an immutable cryptographic signature directly into the compute manifold (model weights, KV-deltas, logits).
- **Steganographic Perturbation:** Uses signal-to-noise modulation that is mathematically invisible to AI inference accuracy (<0.0001% drift) but extractable in constant time.
- **Proof of Provenance:** The watermark is cryptographically bound to the node's AID (RFC-001) and the current epoch, ensuring data cannot be replayed or spoofed.

#### 2.2 Parallel SIMD verification Pipeline
To eliminate the "Security Tax," RPKI offloads verification to SIMD-accelerated hardware lanes (AVX-512/Tensor Cores).
- **Non-Blocking Triage:** The verification pipeline runs simultaneously with the RTTP dispatch.
- **Four-Lane Scan:** 
    1. **Merkle Audit:** Hash verification against the local RPKI DAG cache.
    2. **Watermark Extraction:** Bit-slice extraction from the tensor payload.
    3. **Intent Classification:** Metadata entropy scan for MITM patterns.
    4. **Sovereign Clearance:** Final attestation of AID fingerprints.

#### 2.3 QUARANTINE_PULSE (Instant Isolation)
When a breach is detected, RPKI triggers a reflexive autonomic response.
- **Priority 255 Emission:** A high-priority isolation pulse is broadcasted across the RTTP spine, cutting off the offending node in **< 300µs**.
- **Reflexive Re-routing:** The Aicent Brain (RFC-001) and ZCMK (RFC-004) are notified instantly to void pending bids and reschedule tasks to healthy nodes.

#### 2.4 Swarm Shield (RFC-006 Hive Integration)
RPKI scales defense to the collective level. 
- **Collective Cross-Attestation:** In Hive mode, nodes perform decentralized voting on watermark integrity. 
- **Quorum-based Ejection:** If the Hive (RFC-006) identifies a pathogen by 2/3 majority, the Aicent.net grid executes a planetary-scale quarantine of the compromised segment.
